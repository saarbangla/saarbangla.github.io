Welcome to Saar-Bangla Association

We promote Bangladeshi culture, sports, and history in Saarland and Germany. Our goal is to build a cultural bridge between communities, support Bangladeshi students, and create opportunities for connection, education, and growth.

Our Mission & Values

Saar-Bangla Association promotes Bangladeshi culture, history, and sports in Saarland and across Germany. We aim to build a cultural bridge between Bangladesh and Germany, and support Bangladeshi students and professionals living here.

Through traditional festivals, seminars, and community support, we work to foster understanding, create opportunities, and strengthen connections between our communities.

Cultural Events & Festivals

Experience Bangladeshi traditions through festivals, concerts, and cultural programs organized across Saarland and Germany.

Workshops & Seminars

Join workshops and seminars to explore art, history, and educational topics that support and connect Bangladeshi communities.

Support for Students

Get guidance and resources as a Bangladeshi student in Saarland—from information and advice to community support and aid.

Community Collaboration

Work together on projects that strengthen ties between Bangladesh and Germany. Share knowledge, time, and support.
